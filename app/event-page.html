About Us

私たちについて

Q(WE)R is a series of performances, film screenings, parties, conversations and exhibitions at the crossroads of Europe, America and Japan LGBTQAI+ communities. Making room for them – and us - to reclaim their / our narratives is essential in these times where social links are being particularly challenged. Together with drag queens, scholars, authors, activists and artists, we aim at creating a festive and reflective space to celebrate a united diversity and address the urgency of our togetherness.

Organizer: Q(WE)R — International Queer Cultural Festival

Co-organizer: Ambassade de France au Japon / Institut français du Japon

Partners: TRUNK(HOTEL), The Container, COIL Inc, Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in Japan, Goethe Institute

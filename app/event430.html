FANCYHIM X KOPPI MIZRAHI

Date: 4/30
Time: 22:00-5:00
Location: AiSOTOPE LOUNGE

日にち：4月30日（土）
時間：22:00-5:00
場所：AiSOTOPE LOUNGE

Japan’s most beloved queer party FancyHim, touted to be be one of the best parties in the world, in partnership with Q(WE)R to present a performance by Koppi Mizrahi at Tokyo’s gay district Shinjuku-Nichome during Pride Week.
